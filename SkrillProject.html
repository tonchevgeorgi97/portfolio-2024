Skrill Sports Corner

Type
Mobile app
Industry
FinTech
Period

Nov 21 - April 22

Overview

The Sports Corner was the newest and most significant feature of the Skrill wallet. It gives various sport related winning tips, head-to-head statistics, live score, player and the latest news. It was an important initiative that helped Skrill get closer to its ultimate goal to become the best e-wallet for gambling worldwide. The launch of the sports corner and its positive feedback gave birth to project for a new wallet app onboarding and a personalized dashboard for sports bettors. The redesigned Skrill MVP version was initially launched on the Portuguese market where primary user feedback was gathered through a large scale A/B test.

Design challenge

“Create a new personalized wallet dashboard design for sports bettors worldwide that enables them to have a better and more desired overall payment and betting experience and conveniently manage their personal finances.”

Target users

The target persona for this project is the so-called Sport ritualist. This is a young 20 - 40 y.o. male who is a huge sports fan and bet between 2 to 5 times a week. His main goals are to make informed and well-thought bets, have control over his betting funds and feel informed about what is happening in the football domain. His needs are to minimize the time & cost it takes to deposit & withdraw funds to/from betting operators, decrease the time to learn the most important facts & developments about football and minimize the time it takes to gather the necessary statistics for selected games.

Work process
Research & Define

The design team I was part of tacked this project by conducting extensive desk research. We carried out a series of interviews to develop detailed user archetypes, gaining deep insights into our target audience. Additionally, we thoroughly analyzed our competitors' products and digital experiences. Drawing from our research, we crafted empathy and journey maps, enabling us to define precise job statements and pose critical HMW questions.

Ideate, Prototype & Test

Sitting on an extensive research data we continued our design process by ideating on different low & high-fidelity concepts and UI components that we tested with users to gather important feedback. We took put time and iterated on them until we reached the most suitable user-centered design solution.

Outcome

We created a new Skrill onboarding experience that gathers important data about the personal preferences of the user regarding sports and the financial services that we offer. After a successful registration the user lands on a personalized dashboard with emphasis on upcoming games and statistics. There is information on where and how he can spend with Skrill and what additional financial services we can offer. The user is also provided with additional navigation and explanation of the remaining required steps for him until he is fully onboarded. During this initiative I also explored additional concepts for services that could add value and drive extra revenue such as Sports travelling offers.
